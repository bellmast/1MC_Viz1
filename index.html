Instructions: Check 1MC or FT to display the relevant data. Connection lines can be toggled independently. The legend at the top-left also has rollover features which override the checkboxes.

About: This network visualization maps out the results of two surveys -- one conducted with 1 Million Cups participants, and the other conducted with FastTrac participants. There were three programs of potential overlap -- SourceLink, KCnext, and Startup Weekend. Each survey respondent is tied to programs they have attended in the past. 1MC respondents are in orange, while FT respondents are in brown. Finally, any survey respondents who had not attended one of the three shared programs are fanned out along the bottom and top of the visualization.
